About

Part of Designmatix's broader portfolio.

Designmatix AI is the artificial intelligence division of Designmatix Ltd. — a UK-based design automation company building products that eliminate repetitive engineering work.

Our existing portfolio includes script-based automation for SolidWorks, AutoCAD, Inventor, and Revit — tools used by manufacturers in oil & gas, power generation, and logistics. The AI division extends that mission with a new generation of tools that understand natural language intent.

Whether you're looking for our traditional CAD automation products or our new AI tools, we'd love to help.
